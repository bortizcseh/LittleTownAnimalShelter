Who We Are

At Littletown Animal Shelter, we are passionate about animal welfare and committed to providing a safe haven for cats and dogs in need. Our organization was founded on the belief that every animal deserves love, care, and respect, and we work tirelessly to ensure that our animals receive the best possible care.

Our team of experienced staff and volunteers work together to provide a nurturing environment for our animals while they await their forever homes. We prioritze the health and well-being of our animals and provide comprehensive veterinary care, nutrition, and socialization.

Our shelter relies on the generosity of our supporters to continue our lifesaving work. Donations of time, resources, and funds are greatly appreciated and help us to provide high-quality care to our animals and community.
