Saving Lives, One Adoption at a Time.

Littletown Animal Shelter is dedicated to rescuing and rehoming cats and dogs in need. We believe that every animal deserves a safe and loving home, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality. On our website, you can browse photos and descriptions of our adoptable pets, learn about or adoption process, and find out how you can support our mission. Whether you're interested in adopting, volunteering, or donating, we appreciate your support and decication to animal welfare. Thank you for visiting, and we hope you'll find your next furry companion here!

Did you know...?

  • Rescuing animals gives them a second chance at a happy life
  • Adopting helps reduce pet overpopulation
  • Rescued pets are already fixed, vaccinated, and microchipped
  • Rescuing an animal can bring joy and companionship to you
